"Optimizing Energy Consumption in IoT Networks Using Machine Learning"

IEEE Internet of Things Journal • June 2022 • Impact Factor: 9.5

This paper presents a novel machine learning approach to optimize energy consumption in large-scale IoT networks, extending battery life by up to 40% without compromising data collection quality. Implemented on a testbed of 200 nodes, the solution reduced overall network energy consumption by 35%.

"Security Framework for Edge Computing in IoT Environments"

ACM Transactions on Internet Technology • March 2021 • Impact Factor: 4.7

Proposed a comprehensive security framework addressing vulnerabilities in edge computing implementations for IoT, with experimental validation showing 98% attack detection rate. The framework combines lightweight cryptography with anomaly detection to protect resource-constrained edge devices.

"Low-Cost Sensor Networks for Urban Air Quality Monitoring"

Journal of Environmental Informatics • November 2020 • Impact Factor: 6.2

Developed and deployed a network of low-cost IoT sensors for real-time air quality monitoring, achieving 92% accuracy compared to professional-grade equipment at 1/10th the cost. The system was deployed in 5 cities and collected over 1 million data points during the study period.
